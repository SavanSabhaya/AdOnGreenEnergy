Turnkey Solution
A CBG turnkey solution is a service that helps set up a Compressed Biogas (CBG) manufacturing plant. CBG is a clean, renewable fuel that can be used for cooking, transportation, and electricity generation. CBG is produced from organic waste like food waste, agricultural residues, and animal manure through a process called bio methanation.
Adon Green Energy:
Offers end-to-end solutions for setting up CBG manufacturing plants, including project conceptualization, design, engineering, procurement, construction, and commissioning.
CBG offers many benefits, including:
Sustainable energy:
CBG is a clean, renewable, and carbon-neutral fuel that can reduce greenhouse gas emissions and air pollution
Waste management:
CBG helps manage organic waste and reduce the strain on landfills
Energy security:
CBG can help reduce India’s reliance on imported fossil fuels
Rural development:
CBG can create income opportunities for rural communities
